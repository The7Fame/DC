About us

TS Studio offers our clients all types of Digital Construction Technology and Architectural Technology services from the Tender stage through the Construction up to Project Handover stage. Those services include the followings:

  • Architectural Technical Design (2D/3D)

  • Consultation on the delivery of Project in compliance with Clients BIM contractual requirements

  • BIM and Design Coordination

  • BIM Management

  • BIM Modelling up to LOD/LOI 500 and Management for (AECO) Architectural, Engineering, Construction & Operations stage of the asset

  • Digital Construction Solutions such as 2D, 3D, 4D & 5D
